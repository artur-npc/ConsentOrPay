Consent or Pay — Publisher home Wall

First layer should show the Consent or Pay "Wall": Reject & Subscribe / Login / Accept all.

Test pages: Home · Wall Article · Wall Login flow Subscribed · excluded

Loader

Paste the sandbox loader URL + a settingsId with Consent or Pay enabled, then Apply & reload. Persists in localStorage.

Configuration required (Admin Interface — sandbox)

  • Settings type: TCF (v2.2 or v3)
  • Legal Specifications → Consent or Pay = ON
  • Content → Consent or Pay → Reject & Subscribe link = Stripe Payment Link (test mode)
  • Content → Login link = /login.html
  • Excluded pages includes /subscribed (up to 10 URLs)
  • Service Settings → mark some purposes Mandatory (required for consent)

Expected result

✅ Pass
The Wall renders: a Reject & Subscribe card (with pricing + Login link) and an Accept all card. No Deny All / toggles on the first layer.
❌ Fail
Standard TCF banner shows instead of the Wall, or the Wall is missing buttons/links.

Manual checklist (maps to EUD-5866 AC)

  1. Open in a fresh session (incognito / clear localStorage) → Wall appears (AC 1.1).
  2. Reject & Subscribe → navigates to the Stripe test checkout (AC 2.1).
  3. Pay with test card 4242 4242 4242 4242 → redirected to /subscribed; CMP must not show the Wall (AC 2.2).
  4. Login → navigates to /login.html (AC 2.1).
  5. Open second layer, decline a Mandatory purpose, Save / Reject All → returns to the Wall (AC 4.1, "Reject All" loop).
  6. Accept all → standard TCF signal sent + CMP closes (AC 4.2).
  7. Change language → all PUR texts update (AC 4.3).
